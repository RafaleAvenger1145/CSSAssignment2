Olympics 2024

Olympics Summary

The 2024 Summer Olympics in Paris, held from July 26 to August 11, featured 329 events across 32 sports. The opening ceremony was unique, with athletes parading along the Seine River. The United States topped the medal table with 40 golds and 126 total medals.

Notably, breaking made its Olympic debut, adding a fresh dynamic to the Games. Paris 2024 also marked the centenary of the 1924 Paris Olympics, celebrating a century of sporting excellence. The event was a blend of tradition and innovation, showcasing the best of global athletic talent.
